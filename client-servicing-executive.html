Client Servicing Executive

  • Experience : 0 to 1 years
  • Location : Chennai

Job Description / Responsibilities

  • Ability to think about client’s problems and devise solutions for both online/offline mediums
  • Decoding and communicating detailed, well defined client briefs to the Social Media Team and the Design team. Subsequently, to ensure work is delivered to client on time
  • Understand Analytics to analyze and monitor the effectiveness of various aspects of the campaign such as community, traffic, conversations and engagements, leads and conversions
  • Perform ongoing industry research to identify best practices and better understand Digital Marketing opportunities and trends while providing meaningful value to customer.
  • Arranging meetings and liaising with clients to understand their requirements
  • Supporting teams with the end to end process of delivering campaigns / projects so you must be able to demonstrate very strong communication skills, the ability to multi task and handle administration work
  • You may also be required to support on invoicing so you must be comfortable with numeracy. All this, whilst providing an impeccable service to the client day-to-day
  • Quality control of all work that is submitted to clients
  • Disclosing campaign details to the client and all team members
  • Monitoring all stages of campaigns to guarantee that they run smoothly
  • Writing status reports throughout the duration of the project to keep clients and peers update
  • Ensuring that the project stays within the allocated budget
  • Administration tasks that will help to keep campaigns well-managed and organized
  • Offering creative ideas to the Group Account Manager to develop exciting campaigns and encourage clients to understand the importance of creativity throughout projects
  • Keeping oneself stimulated in order to achieve maximum productivity

Teams to support / Co-ordinate with:

  • Business Development team - for leads/pitches
  • Social Media Team - for clients projects
  • Client Servicing Team - strategies for existing clients, support on campaigns

Knowledge / Skills required

  • A minimum of 6 months at a digital agency. This may have been gained through working in internships within other marketing / communications / digital agencies or perhaps gained client side within a digital marketing team
  • You would be expected to have a good understanding of the basics of digital marketing with the potential and enthusiasm to develop much further within this area
  • Excellent oral and written communication skills
  • First class organizational skills
  • A pro-active approach to creative projects
  • Experience of working as part of a team
  • A keen eye for detail and an understanding of budget restraints
  • Full awareness of creative processes and techniques – including digital platforms
  • Ability to work under pressure whilst maintaining a cool outlook
  • A personable and professional character that will allow you to build client rapport
  • The confidence to give clients trust in your work
